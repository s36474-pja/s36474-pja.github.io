Smithing

Author: mykhailo kovtok s36474

What does it do?

Smithing allows you to craft and improve weapons and armor. Eventually you'll be making equipment so strong that dragons become oversized flying loot containers.

Perks

The skill tree unlocks better crafting materials: Steel, Dwarven, Orcish, Ebony, Daedric and Dragon Armor. Basically a progression from "village blacksmith" to "local factory."

Leveling

Craft items. Lots of items. Jewelry is particularly effective because apparently forging one gold ring teaches more about metallurgy than making an actual sword.

Exploits & Tricks

Use Transmute Ore to turn iron into gold, craft gold rings, become rich, question Skyrim's economy.

The Restoration Loop exploit can create absurdly powerful gear, though it may also create absurdly powerful bugs.

If you're carrying 600 iron daggers, everyone in Whiterun thinks you're an idiot.
