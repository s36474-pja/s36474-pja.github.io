Block

Author: mykhailo kovtok s36474

What Does It Do?

Block allows you to tell enemy attacks "No."

With enough perks you'll become a heavily armed door.

Important Perks

Shield Wall, Power Bash, Deadly Bash and Shield Charge.

Shield Charge turns you into an armored bowling ball.

How To Level

Get hit while blocking. For once, poor life choices contribute to education.

Exploits & Tricks

Find a weak enemy and continuously block attacks. You gain levels while the enemy questions their career choices.

The bandit knows you're farming experience.
