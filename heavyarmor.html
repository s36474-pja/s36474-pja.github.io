Heavy Armor

Author: mykhailo kovtok s36474

What does it do?

Heavy Armor allows you to become a walking castle.

You lose the ability to move quietly, but gain the ability to survive things that should definitely kill you.

Perks

Juggernaut increases armor rating. Matching Set rewards fashion. Conditioning removes movement penalties. Reflect Blows occasionally returns damage.

Leveling

Wear heavy armor and get hit. A lot.

Apparently getting punched repeatedly is an accepted educational method in Skyrim.

Exploits & Tricks

Let mudcrabs attack you while healing. The mudcrab gets exercise, you get levels. Everyone wins.

Many players combine this with Restoration for extremely safe armor training.

If a mudcrab has attacked you for twenty minutes, reconsider your life choices.
