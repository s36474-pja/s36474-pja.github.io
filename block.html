Block

Author: mykhailo kovtok s36474

What does it do?

Block allows you to tell enemy attacks: "No."

With enough perks you'll reduce damage, perform shield bashes, and eventually sprint into people with your shield like a heavily armed bowling ball.

Important Perks

Shield Wall, Quick Reflexes, Power Bash, Deadly Bash and Shield Charge.

Shield Charge is particularly funny because you become a human freight train.

Leveling

Get hit while blocking. Congratulations, suffering is now productive.

Giants are excellent teachers, although their lessons occasionally end in low orbit.

Exploits & Tricks

Find a weak enemy, hold block, go make a sandwich.

The bandit has already figured out you're learning by letting him hit you.
