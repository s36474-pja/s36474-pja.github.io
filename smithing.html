Smithing

Author: mykhailo kovtok s36474

What Does It Do?

Smithing allows you to craft and improve weapons and armor. Eventually you'll forge equipment so powerful that dragons become flying treasure chests.

Skill Tree Benefits

Unlock access to Steel, Dwarven, Orcish, Ebony, Daedric and Dragon equipment. You evolve from village blacksmith into a one-person military industry.

How To Level

Craft equipment, especially jewelry. Apparently creating 300 gold rings teaches more about metallurgy than making swords.

Exploits & Tricks

Use the Transmute spell to convert iron ore into gold ore. Craft rings and become richer than half of Skyrim.

The famous Restoration Loop can create equipment capable of deleting reality.

Whiterun economists have a restraining order against you.
